Welcome

What is this?

While the title is somewhat self-descriptive, in more detail this is a small text adventure reminiscent of some of the adventure books I read/played as a child and probably just as infuriating at times, but that's part and parcel of the experience. Click the central button if you'd like to play and if you have any feedback, please get in touch through the contact section at the top of the page
